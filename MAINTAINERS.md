## Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage issues and feature requests
- Cut releases and publish to Ansible Galaxy / Automation Hub
- Maintain CI workflows and branch protection
- Keep crosswalk profiles (HIPAA, PCI-DSS) current with framework updates

## Becoming a Maintainer

Consistent, high-quality contributions over time may lead to a maintainer invitation.
If you're interested, start by contributing fixes or new crosswalk profiles and
participating in issue discussions.

## Decision Process

- Routine changes (bug fixes, CI updates, doc improvements) are merged by any maintainer.
- New roles, filter plugins, or crosswalk profiles require review from the lead maintainer.
- Breaking changes to CFF schema or role interfaces require a deprecation period of at least one minor release.
